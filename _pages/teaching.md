## Cornell Tech — Break Through Tech AI Program

**Lab Facilitator** — Summer 2026, Summer 2025, Summer 2024, Summer 2023

Led team of three teaching assistants and instructed Machine Learning Engineering lab at Break Through Tech AI. Topics include LLMs, feedforward and recursive neural networks (RNNs), NLP preprocessing, data processing, analyzing the mechanics of decision trees and k-nearest neighbors, trade-offs between algorithmic choices, gradient descent, logistic regression, feature/model selection, bias-variance tradeoff, analyzed ensemble methods (stacking, bagging, boosting), software development best practices, model deployment and monitoring. 

The program helps college women (trans and cis), nonbinary, and other underrepresented groups in tech gain the skills they need to get jobs in the fastest-growing areas of tech: data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. Programming language: Python.
